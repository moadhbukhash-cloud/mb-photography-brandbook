— Merchandise

What the work
travels with.

Apparel, carry, drinkware — wearable and useable expressions of the brand. Pattern accents earn their place; the logomark stays restrained.

— Apparel

Worn quietly.

Heavyweight cotton, single-colour print. The chest mark is the logomark; the cap brim carries the pattern as an inside-only reveal.

— Carry

Tote and bandana.

The pattern hides on the inside of the tote and rides openly on the bandana — two opposite postures, same vocabulary.

— Drinkware

Field-ready.

One enamel mug, two treatments. The pattern band wraps the lower half; the logomark sits clean above. Twelve ounces of capacity.
