— Capsules

Four bodies of work,
one signature line.

The work is organised in capsules — small, edited collections grounded in subject. Each capsule has its own light, its own ground, its own palette extract from the master ramp. A capsule governs a single edit, brief, or installation. Mixing capsules in one piece is permitted only when the subject itself spans them.

— 01

Why capsules.

A continuous archive becomes noise. Capsules are how a wide practice stays legible — to the viewer, to the buyer, to the work itself.

— Edited

A capsule is a closed set. Three to four prints, never twenty. The discipline is in what is left out.

— Subject-led

Each capsule is named for what it photographs — desert, coastline, mountain, city — not for a year, a trip, or a campaign.

— Self-governing

Each capsule carries its own palette, pacing, and pairing rules. One capsule, one edit. Mixing is the exception, never the default.

— 03

The Signature Collection.

A separate tier above the four capsules — hero pieces sized to the composition itself, edition of 30, Hahnemühle Photo Rag only, unframed. One size per image. Reserved for the work that asks to be lived with.

— Format

Custom large-format. Each piece is sized to its composition — no configurator, no choice. The buyer gets exactly what the work asks for.

— Edition

Five pieces total, edition of 30 each. Numbered, signed by hand, certificate of authenticity included.

— Distinction

Lives in its own section of the store — never mixed with capsule prints. Different surface, different scale, different price ceiling.

— 04

Rule of one.

How capsules behave in practice — when they govern, and when they may be crossed.

i
A single capsule governs a single edit.
One brief, one installation, one editorial — one capsule. The viewer should never have to ask which collection a piece belongs to.
ii
Crossing is permitted only when the subject crosses.
A coastline meeting a desert. A mountain seen through a city window. The work itself must justify the mixing — never the layout.
iii
Numbering is fixed. New capsules append.
Concrete & Steel is 01, Shifting Sands is 02, Tidelands is 03, Summit is 04. Future capsules begin at 05.
