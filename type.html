— Typography

One voice,
nine sizes.

The system uses a single typeface family for nearly everything — Inter — paired with JetBrains Mono for technical labels, captions, and timestamps. Restraint is the rule.

— Families

Two faces.

Inter for editorial weight; JetBrains Mono for systems language. The wordmark (custom geometric) is treated as a logo asset, not a font.

A measured ladder.

— Frame · long

Notes on weather, ground,
and the patience of light.

Field notes from a season working at altitude. The work is divided across four sequences: dunes at first light; the wadi after rain; coastline at dusk; and the saltflats, which keep their own time.
