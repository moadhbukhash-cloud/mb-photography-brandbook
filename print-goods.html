— Print Goods

Sellable artefacts
of the work.

Photo book, photo zine, postcard pack, hardcover notebook. Distinct from merchandise — these are works in print form, not branded swag. They carry the photography, not the logomark.

— Hardcover photo book

The body of work,
between covers.

240 pages, 240 × 300mm, matte bookcloth, debossed logomark, sewn binding. The photo book is the long-form. A single capsule across one body of work.

— Photo zine

The short form.

A5 saddle-stitched, 32 pages, 150gsm uncoated, single signature. Released by series: ATLAS, WAHIBA, etc. Each zine is a complete short read.

— Postcard pack

Twelve cards,
one band.

12 A6 postcards, 350gsm, banded together with a slim pattern strip. Each card a single frame; the back carries the logomark and a printed caption block.

— Hardcover notebook

For working notes.

A5 hardcover, two treatments — debossed logomark or full pattern dust jacket. Same book inside; different posture outside.
