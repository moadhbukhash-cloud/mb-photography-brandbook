— Packaging

What surrounds
the print.

Print fulfillment in five touchpoints — the tube, the gift box, tissue and wraps, seals and tags, the outer mailer. Each made from the same monochrome system, sized to a print that arrives whole.

— Photo tube

The tube, end to end.

Mailing tubes are wrapped end-to-end in the pattern. The wrap is the brand experience before the print is unrolled. Pattern at full density; cap in ink; label in mono.

01
Construction
Kraft tube, ink end caps, debossed monogram on top cap.
02
Wrap
Pattern at 100% density, full bleed, paper finish on uncoated stock.
03
Label
Bone label, single line of type. Edition number always present.
— Gift box

The reveal moment.

Long matte black box, debossed logomark on the lid, pattern-printed liner inside. The box is the brand's first physical handshake — quiet outside, loud within.

— Tissue & wraps

Wrap, tissue, dust covers.

Three weights of paper share one printed system. Outer wrap is bone with full pattern; tissue is mulberry with grain; dust covers are soft-touch bone with the corner anchor. All in the monochrome system — ink, paper, bone.

— Shipping mailer

What arrives at the door.

Kraft cardboard outer, white label with logomark, wax seal beside. The pattern stays inside; the outer reads as restraint and care. The unboxing begins here.
